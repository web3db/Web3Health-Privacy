Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-21

Research Context

Web3Health is being developed as part of academic research at the University of Georgia SensorWeb Lab. Data collected may contribute to aggregated, anonymized research insights. Participation is voluntary, and users may withdraw by requesting data deletion.

Data we collect

  • Account data (name, email).
  • Health data that you explicitly authorize (e.g., steps, distance, heart rate, sleep, calories).
  • Device and app diagnostics (e.g., crash logs, anonymized usage analytics).

How we use data

  • To operate the app, display dashboards, and process your chosen sharing windows.
  • To support research participation or partner programs that you opt into.
  • To improve reliability, security, and performance.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with: (a) service providers processing data on our behalf under contractual safeguards; (b) studies or partners you opt into; or (c) when required by law.

Security

Data transmitted between your device and our servers is protected using HTTPS (TLS). Stored data is protected by Supabase’s managed database encryption and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we continuously improve safeguards.

Your choices

  • Revoke permissions in device settings at any time.
  • Request access, correction, export, or deletion by emailing engr-sensorweb@uga.edu. At this time, deletion requests are processed manually.

Contact

Questions? engr-sensorweb@uga.edu
